Meet Our Senior Scholars

Our distinguished scholars bring decades of experience in Islamic studies, theology, and community leadership, each contributing their unique expertise to serve and guide our community with dedication and wisdom.

Shaykh Hasan Ali

SCHOLAR

Shaykh Hasan Ali graduated as an Islamic scholar in 1997 and later earned a Master’s in Educational Psychology from the University of London. He currently serves as Imam at LICS Mosque in North London.

Dr. Maulana Sami Al-Hashmi

SCHOLAR

Born in Vienna, Austria, he earned a Master’s in Dental Surgery from the University of Otago, New Zealand, on a full scholarship. While practicing dentistry, he completed a degree in Islamic Studies from WISE University in Jordan.

Dr.Mufti Abdur Rahman Mangera

SCHOLAR

Dr. Mufti Abdur Rahman Ibn Yusuf Mangera is a British scholar of Islamic and Western traditions. He memorized the Qur’an, graduated from Darul Uloom Bury (UK), and holds a fatwa license from Mazahir ‘Ulum Saharanpur.

Maulana Sulaiman Khatani Bin Yusuf

SCHOLAR

Maulana Ahmed Suleman Khatani, born in Durban, South Africa, completed his Hifz early and graduated as an Aalim from Madrasah Azaadville in 1993. He studied under leading scholars of Hadith and Fiqh.
