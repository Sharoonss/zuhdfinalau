ABOUT ZUHD GUIDANCE

Islamic Knowledge. Real Conversations

Our Mission

At Zuhd Guidance, our mission is to provide authentic, accessible, and transformative Islamic knowledge for Muslims worldwide. In an age of constant distraction, we aim to create a trusted platform rooted in Qur’an and Sunnah — where seekers can learn, reflect, and grow closer to Allah ‫ﷻ‬. We believe that knowledge is more than information. It is light, guidance, and a trust. Our goal is to share that light in ways that inspire reflection and action in everyday life — through lectures, quotes, vlogs, and blogs that speak to hearts and strengthen faith.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital Islamic content platform that bridges tradition with technology, uniting scholars, seekers, and communities globally. We envision a space where:

  • Authentic knowledge is accessible to everyone.
  • Scholars share timeless wisdom with modern audiences.
  • Communities engage, learn, and grow together.
  • The next generation finds safe, inspiring digital guidance.

    Ustadh Saad Al-Hashmi

    Born in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, Ustadh Saad Al-Hashmi pursued Islamic studies primarily in the UAE and Jordan, learning under esteemed scholars such as Dr. Sheikh Musa Furber, specialising in Hanbali Fiqh. He also studied with Syrian scholars through the Taibah Foundation and the UAE Awqaf, as well as other notable ʿulema across various sciences of the deen. Alongside his pursuit of sacred knowledge, Ustadh Saad is an entrepreneur and community leader. He co-founded Zuhd Guidance and Zuhd Store with a vision to benefit the community, support Islamic education, and strengthen the Ummah’s connection to knowledge and identity. He has played a key role in establishing the Maktab at Point Cook in Melbourne, nurturing the next generation with strong Islamic foundations. Ustadh Saad has also been instrumental in bringing leading international scholars to Australia, fostering unity, learning, and inspiration across communities. His leadership ensures Zuhd remains a mission-driven movement that blends faith, creativity, and community service.

    Dr. Sami Al-Hashmi

    Born in Vienna, Austria, Dr. Sami Al-Hashmi earned a Master’s in Dental Surgery from the University of Otago, New Zealand, where he graduated on a full academic scholarship. Alongside clinical training, he actively engaged in community dental programs and public health initiatives, embodying a commitment to service beyond his profession. While practicing dentistry, Dr. Sami deepened his spiritual journey by pursuing Islamic studies at WISE University in Jordan, building a foundation in traditional knowledge. His ability to balance modern medical expertise with sacred learning enables him to bridge science and faith with a holistic, compassionate approach. As a co-founder of Zuhd Guidance, Dr. Sami ensures that the platform’s content remains authentic, scholarly, and accessible, guiding audiences with integrity and care. His dual background in healthcare and Islamic studies positions him uniquely to lead with both intellect and heart, reinforcing Zuhd’s mission of empowering the Ummah through knowledge and action.

    WHY ZUHD ACADEMY

    At Zuhd Academy, we offer a wide range of courses, including Quranic studies, Hadith studies, Islamic history, Islamic jurisprudence, and much more. Our courses are taught by experienced and knowledgeable instructors who have dedicated their lives to the study and practice of Islam.

    We believe that every student is unique, which is why we offer personalized learning experiences that cater to the individual needs and preferences of each student. Our courses are designed to be interactive and engaging, allowing students to participate in discussions, ask questions, and receive feedback from their instructors.

    "Whoever acquires knowledge in order to act on it, his knowledge humbles him. And whoever seeks it for a purpose other than to act on it, it increases him in arrogance."
